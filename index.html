MotionSync 3D - ESP32 Flasher

Connect and flash your ESP32 device to control 3D models with motion tracking

Install Firmware

Click the button below to install the MotionSync 3D firmware on your ESP32 device. Ensure your device is connected via USB before proceeding.

Your browser does not support Web Serial. Try Chrome or Edge. Web Serial access is not allowed in this browser. Check permissions.

Installation Guide

  1. Connect your MotionSync 3D device to your computer using a USB data cable.
  2. Click the Flash Firmware button above.
  3. Select the correct COM port when prompted by your browser.
  4. Wait for the installation to complete. Do not disconnect during the process.
  5. Once installed, the device will restart and be ready for use.
